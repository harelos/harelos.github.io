Privacy Policy

TigerBrands Global · Last updated July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by TigerBrands Global on Google Play, including every app listed below and any future app we release under the same developer account. All of our apps share the same privacy principles: they are private by default.

The short version

  • Our apps run offline and require no account and no sign-in.
  • We do not collect, upload, sell, rent or share your personal data.
  • Everything you enter stays in local storage on your own device.
  • No ads, no third-party analytics, no advertising or tracking SDKs.

What our apps store

Each app stores the information you enter (for example the items, entries, dates, timers or logs you create) locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone through our apps. Uninstalling an app or clearing its data deletes that information permanently. We, TigerBrands Global, cannot see it and never receive a copy of it.

Purchases

Some apps offer an optional paid subscription or upgrade. All payments are processed entirely by Google Play under Google's own terms and privacy policy. We never see or store your card number or billing details. We only receive confirmation from Google Play that an entitlement is active so the app can unlock its paid features.

Data sharing

We do not share your information with anyone, because we do not collect it. The only outbound network connection our apps make is to Google Play for purchases (in apps that offer them). No advertising, marketing or analytics services are used.

Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13 and do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Security

Because your data is stored locally and is not transmitted to us, it stays under your control and your device's own protections.
